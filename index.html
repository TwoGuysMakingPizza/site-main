About Us

At TwoGuysMakingPizza, we serve more than just pizza, we bring the Neapolitan experience to you. What started as backyard fun with friends became a thriving catering business.

How We Run Events

We craft real Neapolitan pizza with just four ingredients: Italian 00 flour, water, salt, and fresh yeast. Our dough is prepared 24 hours ahead of each event for perfect texture: light and digestible yet delicious. Each pizza cooks in just 90 seconds at 850°F in our Ooni ovens. Our all-you-can-eat buffet style keeps hot pizzas coming every few minutes, even for large events up to 150 guests. We will craft together a Menu of 4-5 Neapolitan pizzas specifically for your event. Everything is made fresh onsite, we handle setup and teardown for an stress-free experience.

Our Setup

We're not a Food Truck. Our mobile set up goes wherever you need us, inside, outside, backyard or beyond. No parking, weight or space restrictions. Just a 10X10 space.

Quality

We use only top-quality, authentic ingredients for unbeatable taste, all imported and made in Italy.
