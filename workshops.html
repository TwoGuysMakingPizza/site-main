Book a Pizza Workshop

Interested in learning the art of Neapolitan pizza? Fill out the form below and we'll get back to you within 24 hours to confirm your spot.

What to Expect

1

We'll Reach Out

Within 24 hours to confirm your date, workshop type, and answer any questions.

2

Get Ready to Learn

We bring all ingredients, tools, and ovens. Just bring yourself and your drinks — it's BYOB!

3

Make & Eat Pizza

Learn the traditions, stretch the dough, top your pizza, and bake it in 90 seconds. Then eat together!

Good to Know

  • • Private workshops limited to 10 spots
  • • All ingredients and tools provided
  • • BYOB — bring your drinks of choice
  • • ~2 hours from start to finish
  • • Take home your own pizza dough
  • • No experience needed — beginners welcome
